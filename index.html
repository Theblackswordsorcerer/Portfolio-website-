About

I'm Himank Sharma — a high school student from Delhi with a deep passion for technology, particularly in the domains of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. I believe in learning by doing, which is why I have immersed myself in job simulations, hackathons, workshops, and online courses from institutions like Columbia University, Google, BCG, Deloitte, Skyscanner, GE Aerospace, and JP Morgan Chase.
